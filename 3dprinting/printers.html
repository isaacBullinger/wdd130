Good Starter 3D Printers

Creality Ender 3

The Creality Ender 3 is around $200 and has been around for years. It is a very good printer for the price. It is the most online community supported printer by far. It has a 220mm by 220mm build plate and can print 230mm high. It has a PTFE tube that runs from the extruder to the print head, this is called a Bowden style extruder.

Kingroon KP3S

The Kingroon KP3S is also around $200, it is a relatively new printer. It is an excellent printer for the price. Kingroon offers good support and it has a community online. It has a 180mm by 180mm build plate and can print 180mm high. It has a direct drive extruder that is connected to the print head.
